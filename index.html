Khadi

The Fabric of India's Freedom

Khadi is a handspun and handwoven cloth promoted by Mahatma Gandhi during India's independence movement. It became a symbol of self-reliance and simplicity.

History

The khadi movement began in 1918 and encouraged Indians to spin their own cloth instead of relying on British-made textiles.

Khadi Today

Khadi has evolved into a sustainable fashion choice, supported by artisans and small-scale industries across India.
